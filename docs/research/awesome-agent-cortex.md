## Research intake workflow

1. Capture the source repository and exact commit or release.
2. Classify the resource by architecture layer and district owner.
3. Record license, maturity, dependencies, permissions and network behavior.
4. Identify overlap with existing capabilities.
5. Run a threat and maintenance review.
6. Produce an adopt, adapt, watch or reject decision.
7. Revalidate watched items on a scheduled cadence.

## Evidence requirements

No resource enters the canonical ontology based only on popularity, social claims or benchmark screenshots. Require primary documentation, reproducible behavior and a clear operational owner.

## Decision labels

- **ADOPT:** mature and directly compatible
- **ADAPT:** useful pattern requiring an AGENTROPOLIS wrapper
- **WATCH:** promising but immature or unstable
- **REJECT:** redundant, unsafe, abandoned or incompatible

## Decision

Treat this repository as research radar, not a dependency bundle. Preserve provenance and prevent catalog entries from being mistaken for approved infrastructure.
