# Code of Conduct

We expect participants in this repository to act professionally, constructively, and respectfully.

## Expected behavior

- communicate in good faith
- focus feedback on ideas, evidence, and implementation details
- respect differing levels of experience and technical background
- help maintain a security-conscious and inclusive project environment

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- deliberate disruption of project collaboration
- sharing private, sensitive, or security-relevant information without permission

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, open a private security-style report through the process described in `SECURITY.md` or contact repository maintainers through a private channel when available.

Maintainers may moderate discussions, remove content, or limit participation to protect the project community.
